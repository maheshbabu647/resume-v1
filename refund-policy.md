### Subscription Payments

CareerForge offers subscription-based access to AI-powered tools.

---

### Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only applicable if **no credits or features have been used** after payment.

Once any credits are used or any feature is accessed, the purchase is considered consumed and is **non-refundable**.

---

### Exceptions (Refund Allowed)

* Duplicate or accidental charges
* Technical errors preventing access

---

### Non-Refundable Cases

* Any usage of credits
* Partial usage
* Dissatisfaction with AI-generated results
* Requests made after 7 days

---

### Cancellations

You may cancel anytime. Access continues until the billing period ends. No further charges will apply.

---

### How to Request a Refund

Email [support@careerforge.pro](mailto:support@careerforge.pro) with:

* Registered email
* Date of charge
* Reason
